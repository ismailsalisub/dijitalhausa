Ajami Typing Guidelines

To get the most accurate Ajami script, follow these typing rules:

1. Long Vowels

Use double vowels to indicate length (Alif, Waw, or Ya extension).

TypeAjamiExample
aaَاbaaba → بَابَ
iiِيkiifii → كِيفِي
uuُوsuunaa → سُونَا

2. The Middle Hamza (Glottal Stop)

If a word has a break in the middle (like ma'aikaci), you have two options:

  • Option A: Use an apostrophe: ma'aikacii
  • Option B: Use a capital A: maAikacii
Pro Tip: Using the capital A (e.g., Aura) at the start of a word ensures it begins with an Alif Hamza (أ).

3. Special Consonants

You have to use standard special Hausa characters:

  • ƙ for ق
  • ɗ for ط
  • ɓ for ٻ
  • 'y or ƴ for ۑ
  • ts for ڟ
